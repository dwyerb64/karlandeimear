Our Story

Karl and Eimear met in what is today a normal fashion, online. Delving into the scary world of the internet neither had any idea who would pop up in the 'yes - no game show' that is the fickle world of online dating. However after only dipping the tip of a toe into these murky waters, a mere number of hours, their courtship began. They talked a little, and then a lot and between meeting online one Sunday to meeting in person on the following Thursday their faith was sealed. Little did they suspect, with their first date at Awesome Walls Climbing Centre (where Karl could get in a decent climb if the date went flat).

From there their relationship blossomed taking them all over Europe and America, on many amazing adventures. Their love of travel sparked Karl to propose in Oslo, Norway on December 28th, 2015. Eimear answered 'of course' and as they say the rest is history!!

Travel

Flights

Ryanair fly direct to Malta International Airport from Dublin Airport (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday and Sunday) and London Luton Airport (every day).

Visit www.ryanair.com to book flights.

From the airport:

  1. Rent a car (the Maltese drive on the same side)
  2. Bus transfer to St. Julians - St. Julians is 11km from the airport (17 min drive in car or 38 min bus journey)
