Our Story

Karl and Eimear met, in what is today a normal fashion, online. Delving into the scary world of the internet neither had any idea who would pop up in the 'yes - no game show' that is the fickle world of online dating. However after only dipping the tip of a toe into these murky waters, a mere number of hours, their courtship began. They talked a little, and then a lot and between meeting online one Sunday to meeting in person on the following Thursday their faith was sealed. Their first date was at Awesome Walls Climbing Centre where Karl had planned to get a decent climb in if the date went flat.

From there their relationship blossomed taking them all over Europe and America, on many amazing adventures. Their love of travel sparked Karl to propose in Oslo, Norway on 28th December, 2015. Eimear answered 'of course' and as they say the rest is history!!!

Malta to Gozo

Gozo Channel operates the car ferry that shuttles between Malta’s Ċirkewwa and Gozo’s Mġarr every 45 minutes from 6am to around 6pm (and roughly every 1½ hours throughout the night). If travelling by vehicle you pay on your return leg, when leaving Mġarr (Gozo), so there's no need to buy a ticket in Ċirkewwa on the way out.

Top Tips & Useful Information

The Maltese drive on the left-hand side of the road (same as in Ireland).

Three pin plugs are used in Malta (same as in Ireland).

The Euro is the official currency used in Malta.

Malta is one hour ahead of Irish time.

Valletta is the capital city of Malta.

The official Maltese taxis are white but can be quite expensive and are known for overcharging tourists. If getting in a white taxi confirm the cost of the trip before departure to avoid excessive fares. Alternatively the privately owned black taxis can be hired and offer cheaper set fares than the white taxis.

If renting a car please note you can park on any road, if there is a white line around the parking space. You MUST NOT park in any yellow spaces.

Tipping is customary in Malta. Between 5-10% is common if a service charge isn't already included.
